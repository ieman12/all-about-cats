Popular Cat Breeds

Learn more about the most loved cat breeds, their origins, personalities, and lifespans.

Persian

Persian Origin: Persia (Iran)

Temperament: Soft, quiet, loving

Lifespan: 12–16 years

Round noses with small muzzles and long, luxuriant fur define Persian cats. They like a peaceful surroundings and have a nice, mild attitude.

Ragdoll

Origin: United States

Temperament: Laid back, loving, modest

Lifespan: 12–17 years

Large, laid-back cats notorious for falling limp upon being picked up are ragdolls. They are quiet, kind, and like hanging around with people.

Tabby

Origin: Various

Temperament: Friendly, playful, curious

Lifespan: 12–18 years

Tabby cats are not a breed but a pattern seen in many breeds. They are known for their stripes, spots, or swirls, and tend to be outgoing and affectionate.

Bengal

Origin: United States

Temperament: Active, energetic, playful

Lifespan: 12–16 years

Bengals coats are wild-looking, leopard-like and quite distinctive. They are bright, athletic, and like playing—especially in water!
